What is Five Wins?


Maybe you will know Five Wins as Five in a row. Five Wins its very similar to TicTacToe. The diferrences are:
  • You need 5 token instead of 3
  • The gamefield is bigger

Five Wins is really easy to play and it's very self-evident. Just click on Play in the Navigation and start a game. Befor you start the game, you can choose how big you like your gamefield and against whom you want to play.
